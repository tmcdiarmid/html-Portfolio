Tracy's Three Bestest Films Ever, So Far

I ususally can't remember the names of films so I'll try to go with these three

Bourne Identity (Really any Bourne Movie)

I just really like this group of films for the fast action of the lead character Jason Bourne. Fast thinking and crazy chase scenes make them over-the-top exciting

Invictus

This film had a story bigger than itself with the unification of South Africa on the line after the fall of Aparteid

A Man Called Otto

This film was just really touching and had some unexpected twist that left me scratching my head but also created some strong emotions that stayed with me after I finished the film.
